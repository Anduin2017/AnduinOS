Anekcum i medden akk

Deg wul n tfiluzufit n AnduinOS tella tmedlit belli tasenselkimt tettwag i medden akk. S yifecka n unekcum leqqayen d yiɣewwaren n ubeddel n tutlayt, azenziɣ n yiniten d teɣzi n uḍris, AnduinOS yettarra tasenselkimt teshel i yal yiwen - deg yal adeg d wanda ma telliḍ.
