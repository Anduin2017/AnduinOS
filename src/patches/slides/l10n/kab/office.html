S kra n wayen i tesriḍ deg tnarit-ik

LibreOffice d amazrar n yifecka n tnarit ilelli ideg yella wayen akk i teḥwaǧeḍ i tmerna n yisemliyen, tifelwiyin n usiḍen d usenked. Imṣada d yimasalen n yifuyla n Microsoft Office, ad ak-d-yefk meṛṛa timahilin i teḥwaǧeḍ, mebla tadrimt.
