Zhu s tewlafin-inek/inem

Shotwell d amsefrak n tewlafin anesbaɣur, iwejden i tɣawsiwin-ik·im. Qqen takamiṛat neɣ tilifun i tuzna n tewlafin-ik·im, syen yeshel ad tent-tebḍuḍ neɣ ad tent-tḥerzeḍ s wudem aɣellsan. Ma yella twalaḍ iman-ik·im tzemreḍ ad d-tesnulfuḍ, tzemreḍ ad tafeḍ aṭas n yisnasen-nniḍen n tewlafin deg useɣẓan AnduinOS.
